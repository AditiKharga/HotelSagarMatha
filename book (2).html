Hotel SagarMatha

Hotel SagarMatha is a charming hotel located in the scenic hill station of Darjeeling, nestled in the lap of the majestic Himalayas. It offers a serene atmosphere with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountain ranges. The hotel provides comfortable accommodations, making it an ideal place for travelers seeking a peaceful retreat amidst nature's beauty. Its prime location allows guests to experience the picturesque landscapes, fresh mountain air, and the rich cultural heritage of Darjeeling.
